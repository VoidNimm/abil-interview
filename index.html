About JICC

An international-level coffee conference and expo that will showcase the evolution of Indonesia's coffee industry, which is not just an industry but also part of the creative sector.

This year, Jakarta International Coffee Conference will promote the development inside Indonesian coffee industry. Whether it's about the origin of coffee itself or the supporting products, we will try to cover and discuss it as it's also part of the development of our industry. The event will span 3 days with various engaging supporting content.
